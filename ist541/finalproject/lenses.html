Selecting Camera Lenses

Most DSLR bodies allow you to attach and reattach lenses of different focal lengths. Zoom lenses allow you to zoom in and out through a range of focal lengths while prime lenses have a fixed focal length. Different lenses will allow you to capture different angles and frames of view.

How to Take a Photo Using a DSLR Camera


Step 1: Turn the Camera On

Locate the on-off switch on the top right-hand side of the camera and toggle the switch to "On."

Please note: The on-off switch may be in a different location depending on what kind of camera you have.


Step 2: Select the Camera Mode

Select which camera mode you want to use by toggling the wheel so that the white line lines up with your desired shooting mode

Here's a quick and dirty guide to the different shooting modes:

  • Manual: all settings are controlled and selected by you
  • Aperture priority (AV): you select the aperture and the shutter speed adjusts automatically
  • Shutter priority (TV): you select the shutter speed and the aperture adjusts automatically
  • Program: shutter speed and aperture adjust automatically, but you adjust all other settings
  • Automatic: all settings adjust automatically based on your shooting conditions/scenery

There are also other automatic shooting modes for things like portraits, landscapes, and movement. Try out the different shooting modes on your camera to figure out what you prefer.

For more information about camera settings, check out this Camera Settings Guide by Capture the Atlas.


Step 3: Adjust the Focus Settings

Locate the focus settings, which are usually at the base of your camera lens.

Toggle the switch to "AF" if you would like the camera to auto-focus, or "MF" if you want to manually focus the lens for each shot.


Step 4: Flash Settings

If you are in any of the manual camera modes and want to use flash, push the flash button to raise the camera flash. If you do not want to use flash, push the camera flash back down.

Note: in many of the automatic camera modes, the flash raises automatically; in the manual modes, you need to raise the flash yourself.



Step 5: Take a Picture

With all of your settings selected, you should be ready to take a picture.

Line up your subject in the view-finder and hold the shutter-release button down halfway. If auto-focus is on, the camera should automatically focus on your subject.

Once the camera is focused, push the shutter-release button down all the way. This will prompt the camera to take a picture.

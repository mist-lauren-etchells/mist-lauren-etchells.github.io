Different types of lighting


Lighting is another important factor to consider when taking photos using your DSLR camera. Although you can somewhat control how bright or dark your photos are by adjusting settings like aperture and shutter speed, it is helpful to set up your shot in accordance with your desired lighting.


Two types of lighting that affect the look and feel of your photos are diffused and specular light. Diffused light is light that has been filtered, whereas specular light is more direct light. Click here to learn more about the different types of light.

Shutter Speed


Another setting you can adjust when taking a photo is shutter speed. Shutter speed, like aperture, affects how much light is captured in your photograph, as well as the way that motion appears in your final image. A slow shutter speed can capture more light, but blurs motion, while a fast shutter speed lets in less light but allows for a crisper capture of moving objects.
